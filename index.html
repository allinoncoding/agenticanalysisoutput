Social Disparities: Data-Backed Evidence

Wealth Disparity

In Q4 2023, African Americans had a median net worth of $44,900 compared to $285,000 for White Americans. This equates to a ratio of approximately 6.3x.

Source: Fed DFA – Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, Distributional Financial Accounts (Q4 2023)

Income Disparity

In 2022, the median household income was $52,860 for African Americans and $77,999 for White Americans – about a 1.5x difference.

Source: U.S. Census Bureau, Income in the United States: 2022

Education Disparity

In 2021, 30% of African Americans (age 25+) held a bachelor's degree or higher compared to 42% of White Americans.

Source: National Center for Education Statistics (2021)

Employment Disparity

As of September 2023, the unemployment rate was 5.8% for African Americans versus 3.3% for White Americans.

Source: Bureau of Labor Statistics, The Employment Situation — September 2023

Housing Disparity

In Q2 2023, the homeownership rate was 46.1% for African Americans compared to 75.3% for White Americans – a difference of roughly 29.2 percentage points.

Source: U.S. Census Bureau, Housing Vacancies and Homeownership, Q2 2023

Healthcare Disparity

Disparities are evident in healthcare access and outcomes. In 2022, 8.1% of African Americans (ages 18–64) were uninsured compared to 4.8% of White Americans. Furthermore, in 2021, life expectancy was 70.8 years for African Americans versus 76.4 years for White Americans.

Source: CDC, National Center for Health Statistics – Uninsured Rate (2022)

Source: National Vital Statistics Reports – Life Expectancy (2021)

Criminal Justice Disparity

In 2020, the incarceration rate was 1,186 per 100k for African Americans versus 216 per 100k for White Americans. Additionally, in 2023 (year-to-date), African Americans experienced police killings at a rate approximately 2.6 times higher than the reference rate for White Americans.

Source: Bureau of Justice Statistics, Prisoners in 2020 – Statistical Tables

Source: Mapping Police Violence (2023, YTD)
